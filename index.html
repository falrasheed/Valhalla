About

In northern mythology Valhalla is known as the paradise where warriors arrive after falling in combat. A blissful hall where heroes feast and booze every evening to the beating sound of shields and spears. Today Valhalla turns into a paradise for music lovers who want to get lost in sound and dance. A place to connect through quality Sound, Light & Design.

Since 2017 the Valhalla family, a group of international DJs & producers, have thrown countless events for Barcelona’s underground music scene and provided a platform for a growing number of local and international DJs to showcase their talent. Our concept is simple: an authentic electronic experience focused on quality sound and mesmorizing visuals.

Each night we open the doors to our paradise, we prepare a magical journey through sound to dance the night away. A place for music lovers to feast their senses. Music is the centrepiece of our concept guaranteeing high quality sound while it leads the way for everything else to evolve around.
